Sicilian Eggplant Parmesan

Indulge in the rich flavors of Sicily with our Sicilian Eggplant Parmesan, a classic dish that showcases the region's culinary mastery.

This savory and satisfying recipe combines the earthy goodness of eggplant with layers of robust tomato sauce and melted cheese, creating a symphony of flavors that dance on your taste buds.

ingredients:

  • 3 medium-sized eggplants
  • Coarse salt
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 800g canned peeled tomatoes (or tomato passata)
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 200g caciocavallo or Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Vegetable oil for frying

For Bechamel Sauce (optional)

  • 50g butter
  • 50g all-purpose flour
  • 500ml milk
  • Salt, pepper, nutmeg to taste

Steps:

Preparing the Eggplant:

  1. Wash and slice the eggplants into approximately 1cm thick slices
  2. Arrange the slices on a tray and sprinkle them with coarse salt
  3. Let them sit for about 30 minutes to draw out the bitter juices
  4. Rinse the eggplant slices under running water and pat them dry with a kitchen towel

Frying the eggplant:

  1. in a large pan, heat a generous amount of vegetable oil
  2. Fry the eggplant slices until golden brown on both sides
  3. Place the fried eggplant on paper towels to absorb excess oil

Preparing the tomato Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, heat a drizzle of olive oil and add the canned peeled tomatoes or tomato passata
  2. Add fresh basil, salt and pepper to taste
  3. Cook the sauce over medium heat for about 20-30 minutes

Making bechamel sauce (Optional)

  1. In a saucepan melt butter over medium heat
  2. add flour and stir until a smooth mixture is formed
  3. Gradually add milk while stirring continuosly to avoid lumps
  4. Cook the bechamel until it thickens, and season with salt, pepper and nutmeg to taste

Assembling:

  1. In a baking dish, layer fried eggplant slices, tomato sauce and grated cheese
  2. If using Bechamel, add a layer between the eggplant and tomato sauce
  3. Continue layering until all ingredients are used ensuring the top layer is cheese

Baking:

  1. Bake in a preheated oven at 180Â° for about 30-40 minutes or until the top is golden brown

Serving:

  1. Allow the Eggplant Parmesan to rest for a few minutes before slicing
  2. Serve it warm, perhaps garnished with fresh basil
